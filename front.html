Crop Yield Prediction — Simple Summary

A short, emoji-friendly explanation for quick study

🌱 What is it?

Crop yield prediction means estimating the amount of harvest (like rice 🍚, wheat 🌾, maize 🌽) a farmer will get before harvest.

📊 What data is used?

  • ☀️ Weather (rain, temperature, sunlight)
  • 🌍 Soil (pH, nutrients, texture)
  • 🌽 Crop details (type, variety, sowing date)
  • 💧 Farming practices (fertilizer, irrigation)
  • 🛰️ Satellite images (plant greenness — NDVI)

🤖 How is it done?

Computer models & Machine Learning 📈 analyze the data and give a yield prediction (e.g., tons per hectare).

🎯 Why is it useful?

  • 👩‍🌾 Farmers → plan resources better
  • 🏭 Companies → forecast food supply
  • 🏛️ Government → make policy & pricing decisions
